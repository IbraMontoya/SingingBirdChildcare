Welcome! Are you new to child care? I know that searching for a child care provider can be stressful, but finding the right one can also be rewarding for your family. Finding the right provider will give you the peace of mind that your child is learning, growing and having fun.

At Singing Bird Childcare, your children will experience a clean and healthy environment with nutritious meals for children. Value and the quality of care are all important factors in your decision. You’ll want to place your child with a provider that first cares for your child like you would.

It is normal to feel uncertain about leaving your child in someone else’s care. At Singing Bird Childcare I understand and am sensitive to your concerns, and I am here to answer any questions large or small. Whether you turn to Singing Bird or another child care provider, you need a partner who will work with you and your child to communicate, educate and nurture your entire family’s needs.

I provide after school programs for children whose parents are unable to pick them up after regular school hours. My preferred mode of payment is cash and personal checks. I also accept low income families who receive government aided assistance.
